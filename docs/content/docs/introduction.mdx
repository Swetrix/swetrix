Swetrix is an open-source, lightweight, and privacy-focused web analytics platform. Understand your website traffic, monitor performance, track errors, and make data-driven decisions — all without compromising your users' privacy.

Trusted by thousands of websites worldwide, Swetrix is fully independent, self-funded, and [open source](https://github.com/Swetrix). Unlike traditional analytics tools, Swetrix doesn't use cookies and is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR.

<Callout type="info">
  New to web analytics? Swetrix is designed to be simple. You can be up and running in under 5
  minutes — no technical expertise required.
</Callout>

## Get started

1. **[Create your account](https://swetrix.com/signup)** — Free 14-day trial, no credit card required
2. **[Add your website](/install-script)** — Add a single tracking script to your site
3. **[Verify the installation](/troubleshooting)** — Make sure data is flowing correctly
4. **[Explore your dashboard](/analytics-dashboard/traffic)** — Your insights are ready

Once you're set up, your [Analytics Dashboard](/analytics-dashboard/traffic) will start showing data within seconds.
