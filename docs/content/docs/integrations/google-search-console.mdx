Swetrix offers a seamless integration with Google Search Console (GSC), powering the dedicated [SEO dashboard](/analytics-dashboard/seo) within your project. This integration bridges the gap between **Search Intent** (what users are looking for) and **On-site Behavior** (how they interact with your site).

## Why connect Google Search Console?

By connecting GSC, you unlock a full SEO analytics tab in your project dashboard:

- **Performance Overview:** Track Clicks, Impressions, Average CTR, and Average Position over time with period-over-period comparisons.
- **Search Queries & Top Pages:** See which queries drive traffic and which pages rank best, with detailed metrics for each.
- **Branded vs Non-branded Traffic:** Understand how much of your search traffic comes from brand-related queries.
- **SEO Quadrant Analysis:** Visualize your queries by position vs CTR to identify optimisation opportunities.
- **Search Engine & AI Referrals:** See which search engines and AI tools send visitors to your site.
- **Geo & Device Breakdown:** Understand search performance by country and device type.
- **Privacy-Focused:** View all of this alongside your privacy-friendly analytics in a unified interface.

## How to Set Up

<Callout type="info" title="Prerequisites">

You must have a Google Search Console account with your website property already verified.

</Callout>

1.  **Navigate to Project Settings:**
    Open your project dashboard and click on the **Settings** tab in the navigation menu.

2.  **Go to Integrations:**
    Select the **Integrations** tab from the sidebar menu.

3.  **Connect Google Account:**
    Locate the **Google Search Console** card and click the **Connect** button. You will be redirected to Google to authorize Swetrix to access your Search Console data.

    _Note: We only request read-only access to view your verified sites and search analytics data._

4.  **Select Your Property:**
    Once authenticated, you will be redirected back to the settings page. You will see your linked Google email address.

    Select the correct **Website Property** (domain) from the dropdown menu that corresponds to your Swetrix project.

5.  **Save:**
    Click the **Save** button to finalize the connection.

## Viewing Search Data

Once connected, your search data will automatically populate in the dedicated **SEO** tab of your project dashboard.

1.  Open your project.
2.  Click the **SEO** tab in the project navigation.

You will see a full SEO dashboard with performance metrics, trend charts, search queries, top pages, and more.

- [Learn more about the SEO dashboard](/analytics-dashboard/seo)

## Configuring Brand Keywords

Swetrix automatically classifies your search queries as **branded** or **non-branded** traffic based on your project name and website URL. You can customise this by setting explicit brand keywords:

1.  Go to your project **Settings** > **General**.
2.  Find the **Brand keywords** field.
3.  Enter comma-separated keywords (e.g., `swetrix, swetrix analytics`).

Queries containing any of these keywords will be classified as branded traffic in your SEO dashboard.
