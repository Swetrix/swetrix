## How to link Google / GitHub accounts with your Swetrix account

Connected accounts let you link third-party accounts, such as Google or GitHub, to your Swetrix account and use them to sign in to Swetrix.

To do this, go to your [account settings](https://swetrix.com/user-settings) and open the Account tab. Scroll down to the Connected accounts section, and click on the Link button for the service you want to connect.

A popup window will be opened prompting you to log into the account you want to connect.

After you connect it, you will be able to login to Swetrix using this account. You can also unlink it at any time.

<Callout>
Please note that if you used Google or GitHub SSO feature to create an account, you will not be able to unlink it.

</Callout>

## How to change reporting timezone

When you sign up, by defaut your reporting timezone is set to UTC.

To change the time zone of reports of your analytics dashboard, go to the [account settings](https://swetrix.com/user-settings) page and open the Interface tab.

Under the Time zone section, select the time zone you want the reports to be in.

On the same page you can also change the time format (12-hour vs 24-hour system).
