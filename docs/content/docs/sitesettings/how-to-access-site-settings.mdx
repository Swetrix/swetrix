You can access your site settings in two ways:

<Tabs items={["From the dashboard", "From the analytics page"]}>
<Tab value="From the dashboard">

Click on the 'Settings' icon in the top right corner of site on the dashboard page.

<img
  alt="Settings from the Dashboard page"
  src="/docs/img/sitesettings/settings_from_dashboard.png"
/>

</Tab>
<Tab value="From the analytics page">

Click on the 'Settings' tab in the top navigation bar of your site's analytics page.

<img
  alt="Settings from the sites analytics page"
  src="/docs/img/sitesettings/settings_from_project.png"
/>

</Tab>
</Tabs>
