---
title: Reset your project's data
slug: /reset-sites-data
---

On your [site settings](/how-to-access-site-settings) page, you can reset your project's data. This permanently deletes analytics data while keeping the project, its settings, and Project ID intact.

<Callout type="warn">
This action is irreversible. Once you reset your project's data, all analytics data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

</Callout>

To reset your project's data, click **Reset project**:

<img alt="Invite user" src="/docs/img/sitesettings/reset_settings.png" />

In the confirmation dialog, you will find 3 options:

1. **Reset all analytics data**: Permanently deletes all analytics data for this project. The project itself, its settings, and Project ID will remain.
2. **Reset by date range**: Deletes analytics data from a specific start and end date.
3. **Reset by filters**: Deletes only the analytics data that matches the filters you select, such as a specific referrer, country, or page.
