---
title: How to update your subscription plan
slug: /update-your-subscription
---

To change your subscription plan, log into your account and go to the [Plan and billing](https://swetrix.com/user-settings?tab=billing) page. The billing page shows your current monthly usage, website allowance, session replay quota, API rate limit, current plan, and add-ons.

Click **Manage plan** or **Choose a plan** to open the plan selection page.

## Choosing a new plan

On the plan selection page:

1. Select the monthly event volume you need.
2. Pick a plan type: **Standard**, **Plus**, or **Enterprise**.
3. Opt for monthly or yearly billing.

Standard and Plus plans can be changed through self-serve checkout. Enterprise plans require contacting us because the limits and terms are custom.

<Callout type="info">
  You cannot change the selected plan while a paid trial is still running. You can change plans
  after the trial converts to a paid subscription, or cancel the trial and subscribe again with a
  different plan.
</Callout>

## Reviewing the price

If you already have an active paid subscription, Swetrix shows a pricing preview before applying the change. The preview includes:

- **Due now**: the immediate charge or credit for the change.
- **Next payment**: the recurring amount and next billing date.

We prorate the amount you have paid so far and discount your future bill accordingly. For example, if you are halfway through a 500k events subscription and upgrade to 1m events, the immediate charge will reflect the unused value of your previous subscription. You can read more about proration on [Paddle's website](https://www.paddle.com/resources/proration).

Once you have reviewed the preview, press **Confirm** and the new plan will be applied to your account.

## Updating add-ons

Website add-ons are managed separately from your base plan in the **Add-ons** section of the billing page. They are available for active paid subscriptions and let you buy extra website capacity in bundles of 50 websites.

Increasing a website add-on takes effect immediately. Decreasing, cancelling, or changing the billing interval is scheduled for the next add-on renewal.
